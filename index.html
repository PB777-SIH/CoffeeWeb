Our History

Founded in 1995, our coffee journey began with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest coffee directly to your cup. What started as a small roastery has grown into a passionate community of coffee enthusiasts.

We source our beans from sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, Brazil, and Guatemala, working directly with farmers to ensure fair trade practices and exceptional quality. Every bean is carefully selected and roasted to perfection.

Our master roasters combine traditional techniques with modern innovation, creating unique blends that capture the essence of each origin. From the floral notes of Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to the rich chocolate undertones of Colombian Supremo.

Today, we're proud to serve coffee lovers worldwide with our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community.
